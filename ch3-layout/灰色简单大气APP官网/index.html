Why Choose Whitebox For Your App
(other than saving money)

  • Ready for All Apps - WhiteBox is a great solution for any type of app! Mac, iPhone, iPad, iPod, whatever device your app runs on, WhiteBox has the looks to fit!
  • Slick Content Slider - The WhiteBox content slider allows you to easily create a very slick slide effect with atuto height adjustment for any kind of content! Flash, images, text, audio etc.
  • Bunches of Icons - With 40+ icons, WhiteBox makes it easy to customize your landing page. Quickly change icons by simple changing a CSS class! All the icons come with ready to use tags!
  • Fully Commented Code - WhiteBox comes with fully commented source code, and full documentation on how to use the template so if you are a pro, or setting up your first landing page it will be a breeze!
  • Super Smooth Lightbox - Also included is a really slick, custom jQuery lightbox allowing you to show off highres app screenshots in style!
  • Slide In Subscription Form - List building are you? Then you will love the built in subscription form that slides out beautifuly on click!
  • Standards Based - WhiteBox is a standards based landing page, so no matter the browser or device your visitors are using, your page will always look good!
  • App Site All Star - Become an App Site all start with WhiteBox and get a professional, interactive landing page for your app without the need of hiring an expensive web developer!
